## What Failed

- Free-form interaction notes are too weak for checklist closure.

## What To Avoid Next Time

- Do not close interaction-style rows with shaped JSON alone unless they also cohere with other runtime anchors.
- Do not broaden cross-anchor checks so far that they become brittle without adding real anti-theater value.
